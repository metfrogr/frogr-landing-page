Frequently Asked Questions

1. What kind of jobs can be posted on Frogr?

Frogr supports short-term and flexible local tasks such as delivery help, shop assistance, event support, office helpers, moving help, errands, home assistance, and more. If your task doesn’t fit a listed category, you can still post it as a custom job and receive applications from nearby workers.

2. How does Frogr work for job posters?

Simply post your task with details like location, time, and budget. Nearby workers can apply, you review their profiles, choose the right person, and get the job done — all in a few simple steps.

3. How does Frogr work for workers?

Workers can browse nearby gigs, apply instantly, chat with job posters, complete the task, and build ratings to grow their profile and income opportunities.

4. Is there any long-term contract or subscription?

No. Frogr is designed for flexible, on-demand work. You can post or accept one-time jobs without any long-term commitments or complicated hiring processes.

5. How are payments handled?

Payment terms are clearly agreed upon before the job begins. Frogr ensures transparency so both job posters and workers know exactly what to expect before confirming the task.

6. How do you ensure trust and safety?

Frogr promotes verified profiles, local matching, clear job details, and a rating system so both sides can build trust within the community over time.

7. Where is Frogr currently available?

Frogr is launching in Bangalore first, starting neighbourhood by neighbourhood, and will expand to more cities soon.
