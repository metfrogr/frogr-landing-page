Frequently Asked Questions

1. What kind of services can I book through your platform? What if my desired task is not mentioned in the given categories?

You can book a wide range of services including home cleaning, plumbing, electrical work, appliance repair, carpentry, cooking, childcare, elderly care, pet care, and more. If your specific task isn’t listed, you can submit a “custom request” — our team will connect you with the most suitable tasker for the job!

2. How do I know the taskers are trustworthy?

All taskers undergo identity verification, background checks, and review-based ranking.

3. What if I don’t speak the same language as the tasker?

We offer in-app translation and real-time AI language support to help communication.

4. Can I schedule tasks in advance?

Yes — choose a date and time when you post your task or contact support for special arrangements.

5. Is frogr more expensive than hiring directly?

We strive for fair pricing. Our platform adds verification, escrow, and support — you can compare matched rates before booking.

6. How do payments work?

Payments are held in escrow and released to the tasker only after you confirm completion.
